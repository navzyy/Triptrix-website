Things To Do

Flying Ravana


Location: Ella

Flying Ravana is an adventure park located in Ella, Sri Lanka. It's known for having Sri Lanka's first-ever mega zip-line. This zip-line stretches for over half a kilometer and reaches speeds of 80 kilometers per hour, offering riders a thrilling ride with stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Hortan Plains

Location: Nuwaraeliya

Horton Plains is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is a popular destination for hikers, nature enthusiasts, and birdwatchers. The park is home to a variety of endemic flora and fauna, including the Sri Lankan sambar deer, the Sri Lanka bush warbler, and the beautiful purple-faced langur.

Sigiriya

Location: Dambulla

Sigiriya, also called the Lion Rock, is an ancient rock fortress in Sri Lanka. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country. The rock itself is a massive column of granite that rises nearly 200 meters above the surrounding plains.
